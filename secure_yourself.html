Simeple Ways To Keep Yourself Safe Online

  • Use some type of password generator to make it harder for others to crack your password
  • Don't click on any suspicious/fishy links from random emails
  • Don't share your password to anyone even your own family members
  • Don't give your information to a website that you don't trust
  • Make sure a website is legit/secure before putting personal information into it

More Advanced Ways To Keep Yourself Safe

  • Use a vpn to change your location while in public spaces
  • Create a firewall with a at home server that you are always connected to
