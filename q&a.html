Q: Why should you not use the same password?

A: The reason why you should not use the same password for everything is if someone where to get your email and password for one website they could go to the other websites and use the same email and password and login to your other accounts and most likely sell that information to the dark web.

A: The reason why you should change your password after putting it into a suspicious website is because they can still use that password to log into your account but if you where to change your password the user/users won't have access to your account no more.
